Bio

Holly Jenkins, BS in Computer Science, minor in Applied Mathematics, summa cum laude, 2022 Old Dominion University. Holly is continuing her studies in Computer Science as a master's degree student. She is employed with the (ODU) Virginia Modeling, Analysis, and Simulation Center as a Project Scientist/Simulation Developer where she is working to develop and enhance simulation test environments for the U.S. Navy to test autonomous systems under the supervision of Dr. Yiannis Papelis. Prior to her studies at ODU, she earned an Associate of Applied Science in Information Systems Technology at Tidewater Community College (2018) and worked in the accounting field. In her personal life, she is a mother of two and has a cat named Copper. They happily reside in Chesapeake, VA. She enjoys outdoor activities when the weather is nice or staying inside with a good book otherwise.
